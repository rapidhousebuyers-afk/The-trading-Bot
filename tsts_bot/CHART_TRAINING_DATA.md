### The Logo Sell-Off Pattern (SHORT)

This is a specific, repeatable short setup:

1. **Logo forms on higher TF** (30min/1hr) — consolidation/rounded top
2. **Price shoots UP past lower TF Blue Line** — overshoots to the 5min BL from the 3min BL
3. **Comes back DOWN to the 3min Blue Line** — gravity pulls it back
4. **Goes UNDER the 3min Blue Line** — breaks below
5. **Taps the underside** — retest from below (BL now acts as resistance)
6. **Sells off** → Sell Stop entry at the break of the retest low

### The Logo Buy-Off Pattern (LONG)

Mirror of the short:
1. Logo forms on higher TF (rounded bottom)
2. Price drops below lower TF Blue Line (undershoot)
3. Comes back UP to the Blue Line
4. Goes above it
5. Taps the topside (BL now acts as support)
6. Rallies → Buy Stop entry at the break of the retest high

## Updated Pattern Recognition Rules

### GGG RGG Long Visual Checklist
1. Directional Bars: SOLID GREEN ✓
2. BS Detector: was RED/dimming → now GREEN ✓
3. Bokk: Opening/expanding GREEN ✓
4. Price above Yellow/Gold lines ✓
5. Price above or approaching Blue Line ✓
6. Pink line rising from oversold (bonus confirmation) ✓
7. Orange Line entry trigger: candle close above → enter next candle ✓

### RRR G RR Short Visual Checklist
1. Directional Bars: SOLID RED ✓
2. BS Detector: was GREEN/dimming → now RED ✓
3. Bokk: Opening/expanding RED ✓
4. Price below Yellow/Gold lines ✓
5. Price below or rejecting Blue Line ✓

### Logo Trade Visual Checklist
1. Rounded V (long) or inverted V (short) on histogram ✓
2. Three phases visible: Indication → Correction → Continuation ✓
3. Price interaction with Blue Line on entry TF ✓
4. "Tap the underside/topside" of Blue Line = retest entry ✓

### Blue Line Trade Visual Checklist
1. Price crossing above (long) or below (short) Blue Line ✓
2. Gap between Yellow Line and BS on middle TF ✓
3. Bokk tapering or changing color ✓
4. Lower TF already turning in trade direction ✓
5. Multi-TF combination matches framework above ✓

### Sniper Line Relationships
- **Pink** = fastest, leads the move, oversold/overbought indicator
- **Purple** = "where price will REALLY go", confirmation of true direction
- **Blue** = anchor/target, gravity for price
- **Orange** = entry trigger line (close above/below → enter next candle)
- Pink crosses Purple → momentum shift confirmed
- Blue pulling up (long) or down (short) = continuation confirmed
